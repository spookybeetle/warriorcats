Intro

Warriors

Warriors (also known as Warrior cats) is a fictional series based around the lives of feral cat colonies living in a forest (then lake) near a neighborhood.These cats have sophisticated social hierarchies, political alliances, and religion. The original arc, The Prophecy Begins, was released in 2003, and consisted of 6 books. The most recent arc just concluded, with its last book coming out late 2021.

The clans consist of 5 clans, not including the clan where dead cats go known as Starclan. Thunderclan occupies the forest, excelling at climbing trees. Shadowclan occupies the deep forest, most Shadowclan cats have dark pelts to blend in. Windclan occupies the fieldlands near the forrest. They excel at running, and dig underground dwellings. Riverclan occupies a camp near the Riverbank. A 5th clan is added in a book coming directly after the first arc. This clan is called Skyclan.

Starclan is not like the other clans. This is where cats go when they die. Opposite to Starclan is The Dark Forest (Also known as The Place of No Stars). Cats who have committed heinous acts dwel here after their death.

The Prophecies Begins

The Prophecies Begins is the first arc in the warriors series. It revolves around the main protagonist, Firestar, and his journey of becoming a warrior. The main antagonist of the arc, Tigerstar, is looking to take over the forrest territories. This arc contains six books:

    Into The Wild - Firestar (Known as Rusty at the begining of the book, then later Firepaw) joins the clans
    Fire and Ice - There is political tension between all of the clans. Several battles take place.
    Forest of Secrets - Firestar (now known as Fireheart) wants to know the truth of what happened to the former Thunderclan deputy.
    Rising Storm - Bluestar is unnable to come to terms with her deputy (Tigerclaw, later known as Tigerstar) betraying her.
    A Dangerous Path - Dogs have invaded the forest, and Bluestar no longer believes in Starclan.
    The Darkest Hour - Firestar becomes leader, and has to take on Tigerclan (A mix of Riverclan, Shadowclan, and Rogue cats).

The Broken Code

The Broken Code is the most recent arc in the Warrior's series, and contains 6 books. It revolves around Thunderclan's leader, Bramblestar, being replaced by an imposter, and Starclan no longer communicating with the living clans.

    Lost Stars - Bramblestar has become ill, and is on the cusp of death. Meanwhile, Starclan is no longer communicating with the clans
    The Silent Thaw - Bramblestar is acting strange after losing a life. Loyalty to a cat's clan or the Warrior Code are question.
    Veil of Shadows - Bramblestar is rooting out Warrior Code breakers, and cats now suspect that this isn't the real Bramblestar.
    Darkness Within - Squirrelflight has been exciled, and no one knows where the real Bramblestar is. Tensions are high.
    The Place of No Stars - The imposter puppeting Bramblestar's body has disapeared with Squirrelflight.
    A Light in The Mist - All the clans, Starclan, and the Dark Forest band together to get rid of the Imposter.
