Prefixes

Prefix Explanation

When a cat is born, thier parents, usually their mother, must decide what their kits' names will be. These names will generally end with the suffixe -kit unless otherwise stated by the clan leader. A cat will keep this prefix all their life unless the leader decides a change of name is appropriate. Prefix changes have happened in the book (as noted with the character Brightheart).

Prefix Usage

Below is a chart containing all of the prefixes used throughout the first and most recent (7th) arc in the Warrior's series. Any prefixes that exist, but were not found in either arc have been ommited from data. Click on any hyperlinked prefix to see Actconc data. An asterisk indicates that the cat with that prefix has gone by several names in the series.

Using the frequency of the prefixes, we can roughly track which characters are consistent throughout the book. Prime examples would be Fire, Bramble, Ash, and Gray. These prefixes appear in both arc. When we find what their suffix is, we can easily search for their specific name in both corpuses and see if they appear in one, the other, or both.
